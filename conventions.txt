Naming convention for model names:
    -Term studied
    -Loss fct used
    -Fold number
    -Best epoch number
    -Max epochs to train
    -Optimizer name
    -LR (at least starting LR)
    
Fold number= [0, 1, 2, 3, 4]
Epoch starts at 1 and ends max at Max Epoch
